Welcome

New Horizon is a student-built mental wellness hub focused on approachable resources and community support.

  • Community forum for peer support
  • Event calendar for wellness activities

Our Mission

Our mission extends beyond simply providing online tools. We aim to create a digital space that encourages openness, self-reflection, and growth.
